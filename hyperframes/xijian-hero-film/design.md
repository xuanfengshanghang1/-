## Overview

For the homepage hero of Xi Jian Culture, the motion layer should feel like a cinematic wedding-hall opening: a slow camera push through a gilded ceremony space, soft veil parallax, warm light passing across the frame, and a restrained brand reveal. It must support the crawlable homepage HTML instead of replacing it.

## Motion

- rhythm: `drift-build-peak-resolve`
- camera: slow push, ending close to the starting composition for loop continuity
- light: diagonal champagne sweep with fade-in and fade-out
- parallax: bridal material appears as a low-opacity atmosphere layer
- brand: quiet reveal, never competing with the real homepage H1

## Do's and Don'ts

- Do keep the wedding hall as the first visual signal.
- Do preserve enough darkness on the left side for the homepage headline and calls to action.
- Do keep all service entry text in HTML.
- Do not make the video the only place where brand or services are communicated.
- Do not add loud transitions, confetti, neon, or template-like gradient text.
